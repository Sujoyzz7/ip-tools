Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is your IP geolocation data?

Our IP geolocation data is typically accurate to the city level, with country accuracy over 99%. However, precision may vary based on the IP address and network configuration.

Do you store my IP lookup history?

No, we do not store individual IP lookup history. We only collect anonymized usage statistics to improve our service.

Can I use your service commercially?

Yes, our service is free for both personal and commercial use within reasonable limits. For high-volume commercial use, please contact us about our API plans.

How do I report a security issue?

For security concerns, please email security@iplookup.com. We take security reports seriously and will respond promptly.
